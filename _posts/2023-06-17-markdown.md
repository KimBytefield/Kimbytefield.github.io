## 제목 (Header)

---

* # Header 1

~~~
# Header 1 or <h1> Header 1
~~~

* ## Header 2

~~~
## Header 2 or <h2> Header 2
~~~

* ### Header 3

~~~
### Header 3 or <h3> Header 3
~~~

* #### Header 4

~~~
#### Header 4 or <h4> Header 4
~~~

## 강조 (Emphasis)

---

* **bold text**

~~~
**bold text** or __bold text__
~~~

* *italic text* 

~~~
*italic text* or _italic text_
~~~

* ~~cancle text~~

~~~
~~cancle text~~
~~~


* <u>underLine text</u>

~~~
<u>underLine text</u>
~~~


* `inline code`

~~~
`inline code`
~~~

## 링크(link)

---

* [link](/)

~~~
[link] or <link>
~~~

* <https://KimBytefield.github.io>

~~~
* <https://KimBytefield.github.io>
~~~

* [link](/ "description") description when mouse hover

~~~
[link description when mouse hover](/ "description")
~~~

* [link][Dribble link]

[Dribble link]: https://KimBytefield.github.io

~~~
[link][Dribble link]

[Dribble link]: https://KimBytefield.github.io
~~~

## 목록 (List)

---

  * item y
    * item z

~~~

* item x
  + item y
    - item z
~~~

   1. item 1-1
      1. item 1-1-1 
2. item 2

~~~
1. item 1
   1. item 1-1
      1. item 1-1-1 
2. item 2
~~~
